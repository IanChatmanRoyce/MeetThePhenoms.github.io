Meet The Phenoms

Phenom is a current project of mine, showcasing 12 individual characters, each different in background, statistics, and story.

Set in the year 1980, each character is meant to fit the time period through their looks, which allowed me to perform research on styles of the era according to each character's nationality.

Below are the twelve characters, though this may not be the final number as a smaller roster could be ideal, please include ideas for rosters changes in the feedback section of the about page.
