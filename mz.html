Meet The Phenoms

Mihail Zappas

Height: 5'9" / 175.2 cm.

Weight: 160 lb. / 72.5 kg. / 11.4 st.

Blood Type:

Hair Color: Bald (Black)

Eye Color: Brown

Age: 35

Nationality: Hellenic Republic

Bio: A Greek Olympian who served in the Hellenic Army during the Turkish invasion of Cyprus. A lifelong athlete who trained Olympic freestyle wrestling and traditional

Pankration, missing out on bronze in the 1976 summer olympics. In the service, he was issued US-Nato equipment and assigned as a grenadier, going on to become well

-versed in using his force-multiplier to his advantage. He held his country's pride in his heart and stood unshaken by the change in regime in 1976, as he knew it meant

the better for his country. Returning to civilian life was relatively easy, and he kept training for the foreseeable future.
