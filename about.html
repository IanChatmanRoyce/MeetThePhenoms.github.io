About:

Phenom is planned extensively to be set in the year of 1980, with each character having a different story and campaign to go through as it pertains to their character and nation's culture.

Some examples would include:

  • Jack Ferguson having a campaign centered around the eruption of Mount Saint Helens, and how the explosion was a cover up for a government testing plant housing Lovecraftian-esque horrors.
  • Damir Dragovic having a campaign centered around the Soviet invasion of Afghanistan, using cryptids and myths from Afghani culture to haunt and surround the player.
  • Tetsuya Nakama having a campaign centered on detective work in his old age, and Yokai inhibiting his native island of Okinawa.
  • Thomas Burnes having a campaign centered on the British occupation of Northern Ireland, and dealing with creatures of Irish folklore.
