Meet The Phenoms

Aleksei Ivanaj

Height: 6'0" / 182 cm.

Weight: 191 lb. / 86.6 kg. / 13.6 st.

Blood Type:

Hair Color: Black

Eye Color: Brown

Age: 36

Nationality: United Soviet Socialist Republic

Bio: Russian-born Albanian Farmer who practiced Sambo and Greco-Roman wrestling from a young age, becoming well diciplined in his work. He joined the Spetsnaz

in 1963, serving as a support gunner drilling live fire exercises with his squad. He left the servie in 1968 due to the Soviet invasion of Czechslovakia, where he

saw the extent of what his country would do for a homogenous homeland. After this, he returned to the farming life, quiet and less work or stress than active duty.

Still in shape, he continued to hone his skills in sambo and wrestling, just not at quite the level he used to.
