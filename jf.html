Jack Ferguson

Height: 5'9" / 175 cm.

Weight: 168 lb. / 76 kg. / 12 st.

Blood Type:

Hair Color: Brown

Eye Color: Brown

Age: 28

Nationality: United States of America

Bio: An American-Irish Marine who served in Vietnam as a part of the USMC 1st Division. Participating in Operation Imperial Lake, he fought alongside South Korean

soldiers in CQC operations on Hill 845. Being used for close quarters, he was excellent at clearing bunkers and tall fields. After 1972, he was honorably discharged

from the service for taking shrapnel to the face to save a squadmate. In civilian life, he became a huge fan of boxing and knife fighting, finding himself into trouble

frequently at disco clubs and bars. Eventually he became a pro boxer to chase the thrills of the service, but his heart still belongs to the corps.
