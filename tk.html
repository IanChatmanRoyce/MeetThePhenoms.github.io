Toge Kawakita

Height: 5'6" / 167.6 cm.

Weight: 142 lb. / 64.5 kg. / 10.1 st.

Blood Type:

Hair Color: Brown

Eye Color: Brown

Age: 24

Nationality: Mainland Japan

Bio: Raised in Osaka, Toge joined the JMSDF at just 20, partaking in naval defense drills along his country's borders. Predominantly engaging in boat raids in

which he would deal with the likes of Yakuza and hostage rescue. He then left the service following the loss of a good friend. He struggled to cope, and

dealt with the loss through rekindling his love for Judo and Japanese Jiu-Jitsu, which he had practiced since he was a child. Later, he would study Battojutsu

and become a big fan of baseball, national and international.
